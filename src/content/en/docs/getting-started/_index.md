Welcome to Cloud2Gether! This guide will help you set up your account and start automating your DevOps workflows with AI-powered agents.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Cloud2Gether account ([sign up here](https://www.cloud2gether.com/signup))
- Access to at least one cloud provider (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Administrative permissions to create API keys or service accounts

## Quick Start Overview

1. **Create your account** — Sign up and verify your email
2. **Connect a cloud provider** — Add credentials for your infrastructure
3. **Deploy your first agent** — Launch an AI agent to manage your resources
4. **Issue your first command** — Tell the agent what to do
